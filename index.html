What We Do

Our services.

Light Duty Towing

Cars, SUVs, and small trucks towed safely and quickly. Flatbed and wheel-lift options available for any situation.

Medium Duty Towing

Box trucks, delivery vehicles, and larger pickups. Our medium-duty flatbeds handle the weight with ease.

Heavy Duty Towing

Semi-trucks, buses, RVs, and heavy equipment. We have the rigs and expertise for the biggest jobs on the road.

Vehicle Recovery

Rollovers, ditch recoveries, and accident scenes. We recover vehicles from anywhere, any condition, any time.

Roadside Assistance

Flat tires, dead batteries, fuel delivery, and more. Fast response to get you back on the road without a tow.

Emergency Unlock

Locked out of your vehicle? Our trained technicians can safely get you back in without damage. Fast response.

Heavy Equipment Hauling

Construction equipment, industrial machinery, and oversized loads. Transported safely to your destination.

About Us

Locally owned.
Community trusted.

Graham Towing & Recovery LLC is a full-service towing company based in Baytown, Texas, proudly owned and operated by Don Graham II. Located on Highway 146, we serve the greater Baytown and Mont Belvieu areas with dependable towing and recovery services around the clock.

As a proud member of the Baytown Chamber of Commerce, we are committed to being a trusted part of this community. From everyday breakdowns to complex heavy-duty recoveries, our experienced team handles every job with the same level of care and professionalism.

When you call Graham Towing, you're getting a local business that treats every customer like a neighbor — because that's exactly what you are.
